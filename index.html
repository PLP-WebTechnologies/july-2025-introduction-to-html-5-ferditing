About Us

FreshMilk is a Kenyan startup that connects dairy farmers to urban households through a subscription-based model. We aim to provide affordable, traceable, and reliable milk delivery services every morning.

How the subscription works
  1. Sign up for a plan based on your daily milk needs.
  2. We match you with reliable local farmers.
  3. Receive your milk every morning at your doorstep.

Why Subscribe to FreshMilk?

  • Guaranteed fresh milk daily
  • Supports local farmers
  • Affordable and eco-friendly
  • Easy to manage and flexible subscriptions
